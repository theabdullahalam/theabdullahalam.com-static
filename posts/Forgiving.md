You grew up\
not knowing\
Not every flower\
is plucked in bloom.

Never knowing love,\
You'd wilt out of fear\
from afar.

So\
stop hiding your\
secret love for gloom;

For the world\
is just as forgiving\
as you are.

*-Abdullah Alam*
